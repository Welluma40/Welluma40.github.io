How It Works

From appointment to understanding

Three steps that take the confusion out of healthcare visits.

1

Record your visit

Open Welluma before your appointment. The app captures the conversation — no manual notes required.

Always obtain consent from all parties before recording, in accordance with the laws of your jurisdiction.

2

Get a plain-language summary

Welluma's AI processes the recording and produces a clear, organized summary of what was discussed, recommended, and next steps — within minutes.

3

Access trusted resources

Each summary links to curated, evidence-based health information so you can learn more, follow through on recommendations, and arrive better prepared for follow-ups.

Features

Built for the real world of healthcare

Designed by a nurse practitioner in Northern Ontario, informed by years of frontline primary care.

Appointment recording & transcription

High-quality audio capture turns your visit into a searchable, readable record you can return to any time — not just when you're trying to remember what the doctor said at 2 AM.

AI-powered plain-language summaries

Medical jargon doesn't help anyone. Welluma translates clinical language into clear, conversational summaries your whole family can understand — without losing important detail.

Trusted resource links

Every summary surfaces curated, evidence-based links — from Health Canada to reputable patient foundations — so you know exactly where to learn more.

Secure visit history

All your visits live in one organized, private timeline. Search past appointments, track recurring concerns, and see your health story at a glance.

Share with your care team

Easily share a summary with a family member, caregiver, or another provider. Continuity of care shouldn't depend on anyone's memory.

Follow-up prompts

Welluma flags any follow-up actions from your visit — lab work, referrals, medication changes — so important next steps don't fall through the cracks.

Who It's For

Made for patients. Valued by providers.

Whether you're managing your own health or supporting the people in your care, Welluma closes the gap between the clinic and everyday life.

For Patients

You deserve to fully understand your own health. Welluma makes sure you leave every appointment informed, not overwhelmed.

  • Capture everything your provider says — even when you're anxious or tired
  • Understand diagnoses, medications, and next steps in plain English
  • Share summaries with family members who help manage your care
  • Build a complete, searchable health history over time
  • Arrive at follow-ups prepared with the right questions

For Healthcare Providers

Your patients leave understanding what you said. That means better adherence, fewer repeat calls, and more meaningful appointments.

  • Patients show up more engaged and informed at follow-ups
  • Medication and care plan adherence improves with written summaries
  • Reduces the burden of follow-up phone calls and clarification questions
  • Supports patients with language barriers or health literacy challenges
  • Complements existing EMR workflows — no integration required

Privacy & Security

Your health data is yours

Privacy is not an afterthought at Welluma — it's the foundation everything is built on.

End-to-end encrypted

Recordings and summaries are encrypted in transit and at rest. No one can read your data but you.

Never sold

Your health information is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used to train AI models.

Delete any time

You own your data completely. Delete individual appointments or your entire account at any time.

You control sharing

Summaries are private by default. You choose if, when, and with whom any summary is shared.

About Us

We believe understanding your health is a right, not a privilege.

Welluma Health Inc. is a Canadian health technology company founded in Greater Sudbury, Northern Ontario. We are a clinician-led startup who believes the gap between what's said in a clinic and what's understood at home is one of the most solvable problems in healthcare.

We build tools that bridge that gap — simply, privately, and without replacing the human relationship at the heart of good care.

Clarity over complexity

Healthcare is complex enough. Our job is to translate, never to add confusion.

Patient-first always

Every feature is evaluated by one question: does this help someone understand their care better?

Privacy by design

We architect for privacy from day one — not as a feature, but as a promise.
